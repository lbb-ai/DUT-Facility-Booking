🔐 POPIA Privacy Declaration
Protection of Personal Information Act — South Africa

By registering for the DUT Campus Facility Booking System, you acknowledge and consent to the following in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013:

What information we collect:
  • Your name, surname and email address
  • Student or staff number (where applicable)
  • Phone number and organisation (for external members)
  • Booking history and facility usage records
How we use your information:
  • To process and manage your facility booking requests
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders and notifications
  • To verify attendance via QR code check-in
  • For internal reporting and facility usage analytics
Your rights under POPIA:
  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to object to the processing of your information
  • Right to request deletion of your account and data

Your data is processed by the Durban University of Technology (DUT) and is not sold or shared with third parties except as required by law or for payment processing (PayFast) for external bookings. For queries contact privacy@dut.ac.za.
